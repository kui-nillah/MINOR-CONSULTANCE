ABOUT US

The name of the Company is MINOR CONSULTANCY COMPANY LIMITED, and the Registered Office of the Company is situated in the Republic of Tanzania.
The objects for which the Company is established are:
To carry out consultancy services on information and communication technology, insurance, business, tax management, audit, architecture, research, environment, legal and other related matters.
To carry on the business of advisers, consultants, researchers,analysts of whatsoever kind or nature in all branches of trade, commerce and industry.
To provide consultancy in areas of business development services including business ideas scrutiny, analysis and evaluation, business planning, business formalization, growth, expansion, diagnosis, and renewal in such a manner that the company serves as a business clinic for micro, small, and medium enterprises as well as public sector profit and portfolio organizations.
